Become a member of UniNoter

- and enjoy easy and secure access to study notes from other students around the globe

UniNoter is a novel digital community, where students share study notes with each other – quick, easy and free. UniNoter is for all students across country and institutions and subjects. UniNoter is free and user-friendly.

What is the objective of UniNoter? It is simple! To help students passing their exams by offering a student-to-student knowledge-sharing platform, where students can upload or get access to highly ranked study notes.

Founder of UniNoter, Shakib Ba-Ali

Is UniNoter something for you?

Maybe? You are more than welcome to try it. UniNoter is an open forum, easy and free to join and even easier to cancel your membership.

UniNoter is for you, who is tired of wasting hours on searching the internet for study notes. And after hours of searching you are ending with notes of poor quality.

UniNoter is for you, who is learning by sharing your knowledge and experiences with others. You, who is curious about others work and who is not afraid of sharing your own works with others. UniNoter is for you, who missed one or several lectures or forgot to take notes during the lectures.

Can I find study notes, which matches my needs?

At UniNoter you can share your own notes as well as getting access to high quality notes from other students – entirely free. Study notes at UniNoter are manually approved according copyright protection. The notes at UniNoter are classified according to the following categories, so you can easily find the notes you need:

  • Institution name (e.g. University of Copenhagen)
  • Faculty (e.g. Medicine)
  • Semester (e.g. 5th semester)
  • Subject (e.g. Pathology)
  • Course name (e.g. ??)

You can also find study curriculum, study guides, anatomy and physiology posters, previous examination papers and semester-specific book recommendations.

At UniNoter it is possible to rank the notes by adding comments or giving stars: 1-star indicates poor quality while 5-stars marks high quality. You can utilize the ratings to guide you selecting the note you need.

In order to improve the quality of the ratings we all should contribute with our own experiences. It is the essence of every community work to contribute on an individual basis. Therefor we are urging you to rate the notes you have read, either by adding comments or giving stars.

If you find a study note, which is misleading or contain factually false information, do not hesitate to contact us by clicking at the “feedback” button under the actual note.

Data privacy and security

UniNoter manages your personal data according to the current EU data protection law including The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). When you register by your Facebook account or G-mail will you directed to UniNoter though an encrypted and secure connection to UniNoter. UniNoter has only access to the information you have provided in your profile.
